  • Then, to start game you select the map where you will play.
  • You get 50 scores for your ships and 0 for enemy ships. Click on the ship to shoot it. When you shoot your ship the number of your ships reduces, shooting an enemy ship increase the score of destroyed enemy ships.
    The total score is calculated as your ships plus destroyed enemy ships.
    The maximum score is 100.
